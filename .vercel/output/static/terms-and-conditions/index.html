Visa, APS, & Immigration

Securing a student visa is a complex legal process governed entirely by the laws of the destination country.

The issuance of the VISA is NOT GUARANTEED. It involves a personal interview by the concerned Embassy according to policy restrictions.

We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party bodies like APS India, Uni-Assist, CIMEA, or Universitaly.

In the event of a visa rejection, Finesse shall not be held financially or legally liable. The applicant bears full responsibility for providing authentic financial and personal documents.
