Strict Govt Rules

NMC 2026 Official Rules

The National Medical Commission has strict rules for students going abroad. We physically check colleges to ensure 100% safety.

The 54 + 12 Months Rule

Course duration must be strictly 54 months, followed by a mandatory 12-month internship in the same foreign country.

100% English Medium

Bilingual (Two languages) courses are not accepted. The entire medical syllabus must be taught in English.

Licensing & Registration

The student must be eligible to get a valid medical practice license in that country after graduation.
